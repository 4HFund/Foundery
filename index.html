Why We’re Here

Rising costs shouldn’t steal anyone’s dignity. After hearing a challenge to “use the gifts God gave you,” Barbra and Jay Beatty turned their retail know-how into a ministry. Kenny’s Kloset honours the legacy of the late Kenneth “Kenny” Hart—beloved Brooke County teacher, principal and coach—by offering affordable essentials to every neighbour, no strings attached. All proceeds circle right back into helping local families, so every dollar you spend sparks another act of kindness.

What is Kenny’s Kloset?

Kenny’s Kloset offers affordable clothing to individuals and families in our community. For just $1 per item, you can fill up on quality clothes for yourself or your loved ones— no questions, no judgment, just kindness.

What You’ll Find:

  • Clothing for men, women & children
  • Shoes & seasonal wear
  • Clean, organized space
  • Friendly volunteers happy to help

Open Hours:

1st and 3rd Saturday of every month
No appointment needed – come as you are!

Want to Help?

We gladly accept gently used clothing donations. Drop off during open hours or call to schedule a pickup. Volunteers welcome—be part of something good!

What We Believe

Explore the core beliefs that shape our mission, our ministry, and our message at The Foundery Church.

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God, guiding our faith and daily lives.

God in Three Persons

We believe in one God, eternally existent in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

Jesus Christ

We believe Jesus is the Son of God, sent to redeem humanity through His life, death, and resurrection.

The Holy Spirit

The Holy Spirit empowers, guides, and comforts believers, working actively in our world today.

The Church

We believe the Church is the body of Christ on earth, called to share the Gospel and serve in love.

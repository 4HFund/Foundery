Our Purpose

Kenny’s Kloset began as a simple dream from Barb Beatty—to make sure no one in Brooke County had to choose between clothing and other essentials. Named in loving memory of her brother Kenny, whose generous heart inspired this ministry, we offer gently used clothing for just $1 per item. Hosted inside The Foundery Church, every rack is a reminder that dignity should never be expensive.
